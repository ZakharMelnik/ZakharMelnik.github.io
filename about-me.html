About me
Why is it worth working with me? You can work for a long time, but you can work quickly. I am a professional and I do it quickly without losing quality.

My goal is to create a website that will display well on any device without overloading the system. The client wants to go to the site and get the result he needs without wasting extra time.

I have taken theoretical web developer courses from Glo Academy, studied documentation, followed trends. I practiced in a young and energetic team, where the main goal is to show the desired result within the specified time frame. After a long collaboration, I took up freelancing and continued to improve my skills. Behind my shoulders is a wide variety of sites of different specifics, different levels of complexity and volume.

So why aren't we working together yet?
